        Tally Prime – Silver & Gold Editions

        Tally Prime is a complete business management software that simplifies accounting, invoicing, inventory, banking, compliance, and reporting for businesses of all sizes.

        Tally Prime Silver

        Tally Prime Silver is designed for single-user businesses that require a complete, reliable, and easy-to-use business management solution. It enables business owners to manage accounting, invoicing, inventory, banking, and statutory compliance from a single, integrated platform.

        • • End-to-end invoicing and accounting operations
        • • Inventory and stock movement tracking
        • • VAT-compliant transactions and reporting
        • • Banking, reconciliation, and cashflow management
        • • e-Invoicing and statutory compliance features
        • • MIS, business reports, and financial insights

        Tally Prime Gold

        Tally Prime Gold is designed for growing organizations that require multiple users to work simultaneously with shared and centralized business data. It enables secure collaboration across departments, branches, and locations while maintaining full control over access and operations.

        • • Unlimited concurrent multi-user access
        • • Centralized accounting and data management
        • • Multi-branch and multi-location business support
        • • Advanced user roles, permissions, and controls
        • • Real-time collaboration across teams
        • • Scalable solution aligned with business growth
        Performance & Infrastructure

        Tally Prime Server – Silver & Gold

        Tally Prime Server enhances performance, stability, and data control by hosting Tally Prime on a dedicated server environment, ensuring smooth operations and reliable access for structured business setups.

        Tally Prime Server – Silver

        Tally Prime Server Silver is designed for businesses using Tally Prime Silver that require centralized data storage and improved system performance. It ensures better control over business data while maintaining a stable and secure working environment.

        • • Centralized data storage on a dedicated server
        • • Improved performance over standalone installations
        • • Reduced risk of data corruption
        • • Secure on-premise server deployment
        • • Recommended for structured office environments
        • • Compatible with Tally Prime Silver

        Tally Prime Server – Gold

        Tally Prime Server Gold is built for multi-user organizations that require high performance, reliability, and centralized data access. It supports concurrent users efficiently while ensuring data security and operational stability.

        • • Dedicated server for multi-user environments
        • • High-speed concurrent user performance
        • • Centralized and secure data management
        • • Enhanced stability for large transaction volumes
        • • Reduced network latency and downtime
        • • Ideal for growing and enterprise organizations
