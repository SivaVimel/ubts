Products

Tally Prime

Tally Prime is the flagship product offered by United Business Trading & Services LLC (BTS). It is a comprehensive business management and accounting solution designed to meet the operational, compliance, and reporting requirements of businesses across multiple industries.

With over three decades of experience working with Tally Solutions in the Sultanate of Oman, BTS understands how Tally Prime can be implemented, configured, and optimized to suit real-world business needs.

Key Features

  • Accounting & financial management
  • Inventory & stock control
  • VAT compliance & e-invoicing
  • Multi-company and multi-branch support
  • Real-time MIS & statutory reports

Business Coverage

  • Trading & distribution companies
  • Manufacturing units
  • Engineering & construction firms
  • Finance-driven organizations
  • Group & branch-based operations

Tally Prime can be implemented across multiple business units and accessed from branches, enabling better control, visibility, and decision-making across the organization.

Tally TDL (Customization)

Every business operates differently. To address specific operational and reporting needs, BTS offers customization services using Tally Definition Language (TDL).

Through TDL, Tally Prime can be enhanced with customized reports, transactions, and controls, ensuring the system aligns closely with the client’s internal processes and management requirements.

Customization Areas

  • Customized MIS & management reports
  • Industry-specific formats & summaries
  • Transaction-level validations
  • Additional fields & data capture

Benefits to Business

  • Improved reporting accuracy
  • Better visibility of business performance
  • Reduced manual work & adjustments
  • ERP system aligned to actual operations

BTS works closely with experienced TDL developers to ensure that all customizations are stable, scalable, and compatible with future upgrades of Tally Prime.

About BTS

Our Journey

United Business Trading & Services LLC (BTS), formerly known as Business Tools & Solutions LLC, is a trusted IT solutions provider in the Sultanate of Oman. Established in 1996, BTS has been delivering accounting and business management solutions for nearly three decades.

With a strong focus on Tally Prime, BTS has supported businesses of all sizes by helping them adopt reliable, compliant, and scalable accounting systems aligned with real operational needs.

Our Association with Tally

BTS has been closely associated with Tally Solutions for over 30 years. As an authorized Tally partner in Oman, we have been involved in implementation, support, customization, and continuous upgrade services for Tally users across industries.

This long-standing association allows us to understand both the technical capabilities of Tally Prime and the practical challenges faced by businesses using it on a daily basis.
